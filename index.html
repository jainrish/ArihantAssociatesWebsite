about

Arihant Associates was formed in the year 2010 to provide superior quality boards and sealing compounds to the people across the market. Ever since our formation, we have been growing tremendously as a manufacturer and supplier of high quality Joint Filler Boards, Allied Bitumen Products, Expansion Joint Filler Boards, Bitumen Boards, Shalitex Boards and Sealing Compounds. Using the supreme grade cane fibre, bitumen impregnated and water resistant materials, these are made by our skilled workers in accordance with the industry defined quality norms. The boards we offer are extensively used for filling the gaps created in the expansion joints due to their outstanding recovery characteristics and high resistance to compression. Leveraging on the hard efforts of our personnel, we are attaining tremendous growth and popularity in the domain. Further, our emphasis on research & development tasks and transparency in the deals have helped us in mustering a huge clientele, globally.We are dealing Pan India.
